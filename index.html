About Me

I'm a design leader who helps teams focus on the right problems and turn ambiguity into clear direction. I've spent the last decade building and leading design teams at scale, working across product, design, and strategy.

What drives me is making complexity manageable: aligning people around a shared understanding of the customer, raising the bar on craft, and helping organizations move from good intentions to real outcomes. Not just shaping interfaces, but shaping decisions, priorities, and how teams learn.

Originally from New York, now based in Amsterdam. When I'm not working, I'm usually running, traveling, exploring new food and music, or out in nature with my dog, Oreo.
