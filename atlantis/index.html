Case Studies

Automating the Filing of a Customs Entry

Flexport | Design Manager | March 2023 - November 2023

I led a 3-person UX team that envisioned and executed a 0 to 1 product for the design of a new platform to automate customs entry. The platform was designed to automate all possible tasks when preparing and filing a customs entry, and to escalate a task to the right person (customs broker, partner, customer) if the automation failed. Our goal was to reduce the cost to serve for an entry by 50%, allowing the company to scale its operations and take on new business.

Project Polaris - Vision for the Customs Workspace

Flexport | Design Manager | January 2022 - June 2022

I initiated and partnered with business, product, and tech stakeholders to lead the creation of a UX vision for our business area which identified 7 key opportunities for us to increase our customs brokers efficiency and satisfaction score. We implemented 11 of the product concepts from the vision and have included 7 of the concepts in our long term product strategy.

Long Stay Weekly & Monthly Discount

Booking.com | Lead Product Designer | May 2020 - September 2020

I led a UX team (2 UX designers, 1 UX copywriter, 1 researcher) in creating an MVP that enabled our accommodation partners to offer weekly and monthly stay rate plans. The purpose of this initiative was to enable accommodation partners to provide availability and competitive prices so that they could offer customers the best price.

Onboarding New Accommodation Partners

Booking.com | Lead Product Designer | May 2019 - August 2020

I created the strategy for the guidance framework that guided our inexperienced partners through five key milestones of their onboarding journey and led a UX team (1 UX designer, 1 UX copywriter, 1 UX researcher) to bring it to life. The goal of this initiative was to educate and guide partners through these early milestones to become profitable partners and create memorable experiences for their guests.

Reusable PGA Product Profiles

Flexport | Senior Product Designer | March 2022 - July 2022

I designed an end to end workflow that enabled our customs brokers to create reusable profiles for products so that they didn’t have to fill in the same information each time a company shipped the same product. This reduced our customs brokers' cost to serve by enabling them to file more entries per month.
